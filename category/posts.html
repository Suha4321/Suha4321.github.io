AirBnB listing in Jersey city¶

I have been hosting AirBnB in my home basement as a part time passive income source. Things have been easy after the first set up. I live with my daughter and have to be very careful and selective about my guests. I noticed that I have been turning down many guests because of this. One of my friends suggested to open up a full time AirBnb business. In order to do so, I need to set up AirBnB in areas that have a higher visitor footfall. One of such areas is Jersey City. In this post I want to find out what areas in Jersey city have potential and what might be the average going rate for my listing.

The data for jersey city listing was taken from http://insideairbnb.com/get-the-data.html we are using the summary data - 'listing' for analysis.

To understand the data a little better, tom slee has explained the column headers in this post - http://tomslee.net/airbnb-data-collection-get-the-data
